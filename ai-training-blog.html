AI for Everyone: Harnessing the Power of Artificial Intelligence in Sierra Leone

Announcements September 2025

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming the way the world works — from healthcare and education to business and agriculture. At Code4ImpactSL, we believe Sierra Leone must not be left behind in this global shift. That's why we are launching our AI Awareness Training, designed for people from all walks of life.

This initiative will help participants understand, explore, and apply AI tools in their daily lives and businesses. The training is structured to be practical, inclusive, and beginner-friendly, ensuring that even those without technical backgrounds can benefit.

Participants will learn how AI can:

  • Boost productivity in business and marketing.
  • Improve agriculture through smart farming techniques.
  • Support healthcare with data-driven insights.
  • Enhance education with personalized learning tools.

At the end of the training, participants will not only receive a certificate of participation but also walk away with practical skills to apply AI solutions in their work and communities.

As the president of Code4ImpactSL, I see this as a defining moment for our organization and our country. By raising awareness and providing access to AI knowledge, we are helping Sierra Leoneans become creators, not just consumers, of technology.

The future belongs to those who can adapt and innovate — and with the right skills, Sierra Leone can take its rightful place in the digital economy.
