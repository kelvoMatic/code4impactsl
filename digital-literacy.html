Digital Literacy & Inclusive Education

Building a tech-savvy generation

We equip students and teachers with practical digital skills and resources so every child—urban or rural—can participate in Sierra Leone's digital future.

Our Programs

Code4Kids

Introduces coding in schools through structured, beginner-friendly modules, starting with pilots and scaling across districts. This program makes computer science accessible to children aged 8-14, fostering early interest in technology and problem-solving.

Girls in Tech SL

Our flagship gender-equity initiative providing hands-on coding, robotics, mentorship, and leadership opportunities. We're committed to closing the gender gap in technology by empowering young women with the skills and confidence to pursue tech careers.

Digital Learning Hubs

Offline libraries and resource centers that deliver curated digital curricula where internet connectivity is limited. These hubs ensure that rural communities have access to quality digital education resources regardless of connectivity challenges.

Our Impact

Through these programs, we're creating a foundation for digital literacy that will enable Sierra Leone's youth to compete in the global digital economy. Our inclusive approach ensures that no child is left behind, regardless of their location or background.
