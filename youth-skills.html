Youth Skills & Digital Employment

Turning skills into opportunities

We prepare young people for the digital economy with marketable skills, portfolio development, and pathways to freelance and local employment.

Our Programs

Youth Freelance Empowerment

Short, intensive bootcamps covering web development, design, soft skills, portfolio building, and onboarding to freelance platforms with ongoing mentorship. This program equips youth with the technical and business skills needed to succeed in the global freelance marketplace.

Code4Impact Festival

Annual showcase where youth present projects, network with employers and partners, and access internships, gigs, and awards. This event serves as a bridge between talented young developers and potential employers, creating real opportunities for career advancement.

Our Impact

By focusing on practical, marketable skills and providing direct pathways to employment, we're helping Sierra Leone's youth transition from learning to earning. Our programs create immediate economic opportunities while building a foundation for long-term career success in the digital economy.
